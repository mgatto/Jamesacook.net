James A. Cook is a sculptor and currently chairs the 3-D Division in the School of Art at the University of Arizona. His sculpture and video work have been extensively exhibited nationally and internationally, and he has participated in residencies, conferences, and symposia in the United States, Spain, India, Nepal, Japan, and Bulgaria.

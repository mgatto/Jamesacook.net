Tsu’s Bridge

bronze, copper

18″ × 68″ × 22″

A old copper clad kettle appears to hover inches above the ground, supported by the long, extended rhizomes of bamboo. The kettle contains a liquid through courses more rhizomes. That which supports is also conveyed in a circular relationship, referring to the connectivity of inner and outer dynamics of mind.

Conundrum Abode (adobe)

Adobe, steel, LCD monitor, DVD player, video recording
48″ × 22″ × 22″

Our circumstance as human beings dictates that we must discover or construct our own cosmological meaning. Our decisions and actions at times bear fruit and at other moments seem bereft and leave us in states of relative isolation. It may be that we are at once both alone and connected. In this work a rational stability is suggested by the adobe block construction as an isolated individual attempts to advance without seeming to progress.

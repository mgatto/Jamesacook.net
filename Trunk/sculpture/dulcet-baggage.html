Dulcet Baggage

bronze
38″ × 42″ × 27″

A bronze cast ‘cardboard’ box with flaps open at one end is situated next to a child’s box guitar, also cast in bronze. The interiors of each echo their respective outer forms—one rather existential and the other with prepubescent hope.
